A little about Galway Leisure Centre

Galway Leisure Centre has been situated at the heart of Salthill, along the spectacular promenade since 1973. Located along the Wild Atlantic Way, Galway Leisure Centre is a state-of-the-art facility which includes, a 25m pool, a learner pool, leisure pool with a thrilling 65m water slide and giant Commando Run Inflatable Obstacle Course and a fully equipped gymnasium. Open daily, with ample free parking and a delicious coffee dock, Galway Leisure Centre is the ideal spot for a perfect family fun day no matter what the weather.
